“Ever fantasized about creating your own IPL team,now is the chance, make the most of it!”

IPL is the most attended and watched cricket league in the world. It provides a platform to the youngsters as they get a chance to showcase their talent in front of the entire world. Impact players from different countries get an opportunity to play for the same franchise.
Here is your chance to create your own IPL team and grab exciting prizes. You all must have surly dreamt of it.

1. There will be a quiz competition it will test your knowledge of the most exciting cricket league in the world.

3. Registrations will be done online. Top eight(8) teams will be selected for the auction round.
4. Details and rules of the auction round will be told to you then and there itself.

So enjoy the thrill of owning an IPL team and have a chance to win exciting prizes.
